Erica works at Eaze, which is a technology platform in California that facilitates marijuana delivery. Erica herself got a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science and is really interested in Android application development. They talk about what it’s like working at a big company like Amazon or Walmart versus Eaze, her general overview of working with React Native, and some Android-related pitfalls that they had. They also touch on her experience with the React Native maps library, if they looked at other cross-platform options before deciding to use React Native, and more!
