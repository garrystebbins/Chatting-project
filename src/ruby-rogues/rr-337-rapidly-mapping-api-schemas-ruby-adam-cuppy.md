 **Panel:** Brian Hogan Dave Kimura Eric Berry **Special Guest:&nbsp;** Adam Cuppy In this episode, the Ruby Rogues speaks with Adam Cuppy. Adam is the co-founder of [Zeal](https://codingzeal.com).
